Change readiness for your leadership teams – and your organization

We work with HR and People teams to strengthen change readiness across your leadership teams.

The focus is on how leadership teams actually operate when it matters. How they think, decide, collaborate, and learn when pressure rises, priorities shift, and new ways of working emerge.

Our work builds on two core perspectives.

Growth Mindset 2.0
A focus on behavior under pressure and how leadership teams operate in real situations.

Human+AI
A focus on how work is changing and what this requires from leaders and teams in practice, including how decisions are made, how collaboration evolves, and how learning happens in everyday work.

We bring tools, interactions, and structured ways of working. Together with you, we shape these into something that fits your context and supports your leadership teams in their day-to-day work.

This often develops into your own internal set of tools and ways of working, supported by people in your organization who can take this forward over time.

Theory

Growth Mindset 2.0 and Human+AI are the two perspectives behind this work.

Growth Mindset 2.0 focuses on change readiness. It looks at how leadership teams think and act when situations are unclear, when pressure increases, and when outcomes do not go as planned.

Human+AI focuses on how work is evolving. This includes how people make decisions, collaborate, share knowledge, and learn in new ways as AI becomes part of everyday work.

Tools & Interactions

This is the practical core of the work.

We bring a growing library of tools and assessments, leadership situations, and Team Dynamics Card-style interactions.

These are used to support leadership teams in real situations. Leaders work with how decisions are made when things are unclear, how pressure is handled, how collaboration works across functions, and how learning is built from what actually happens.

Over time, this becomes your internal library. Something your organization can use, develop, and build on.

Learning Programs

Learning is built from the tools and interactions.

We shape learning together with you, based on your priorities, your leadership challenges, and the situations your teams are dealing with.

This often includes Growth Mindset 2.0 for Leadership Teams, AI for Leadership Teams, or a combination of both.

The aim is to keep learning close to the work itself. Real situations, shared language, and a clear link between reflection and action.

Train-the-Trainer

This is a central part of how we work with HR and People teams.

We support you in building the internal capability to run and facilitate this work, adapt tools to your context, and scale the approach across leadership teams.

This includes developing your own facilitators and building confidence in how the tools are used in real situations.

Over time, this becomes part of how your organization works with leadership development. Your teams can run it, adjust it, and build on it as your needs evolve.
