Learning Programs

Build tailored learning programs from practical tools

Learning Programs combine theory, tools, leadership situations, reflection, and action. The aim is to help leadership teams work with real challenges, not just complete content.

Growth Mindset 2.0 for Leadership Teams

A learning program focused on change readiness, behavior under pressure, mistakes and learning, cross-functional collaboration, and how mindset becomes visible in the way leadership teams work.

The program can be built from tools such as Gap Map, Expand the Comfort Zone, Define Growth Mindset in Context, leadership situations, and action builders.

AI for Leadership Teams

A learning program focused on AI as a leadership and team development challenge. The focus is not only tools and technology, but how people work, decide, collaborate, and learn as AI becomes part of everyday work.

This can include Human + AI theory, readiness assessments, leadership situations, team discussions, and practical experiments.

Build your own program

The longer-term direction is a click-and-mix approach. Choose the tools, interactions, theory elements, assessments, and action builders that fit your organization’s situation, and combine them into a tailored learning program.

This is especially relevant for HR and People teams that want to create something practical, flexible, and adapted to their own leadership context.
