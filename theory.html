Growth Mindset 2.0 and Human+AI

Growth Mindset 2.0 and Human+AI are the two perspectives behind this work.

Together, they help leadership teams understand how behavior, learning, decision-making, and collaboration need to evolve in real work.

Growth Mindset 2.0

Growth Mindset 2.0 focuses on change readiness. It looks at how leadership teams think and act when situations are unclear, when pressure increases, and when outcomes do not go as planned.

It builds on the original growth mindset idea, but brings the focus closer to real leadership behavior. How teams respond to uncertainty. How they work with mistakes. How they stay open when decisions are difficult.

The visual overview introduces Growth Mindset Essentials, the mindset zones, and what this means for leadership teams in 2026.

Human+AI

Human+AI focuses on how work is evolving as AI becomes part of everyday work.

This includes how people make decisions, collaborate, share knowledge, and learn in new ways. It also asks what leaders and teams need to practice as AI changes workflows, roles, and expectations.

The focus is practical. How do leadership teams build the change readiness, habits, and ways of working needed to use AI in meaningful ways?

A Toolbook for Your Company

One way to make the theory practical is to turn it into a tailored toolbook for your organization.

A toolbook can combine short theory, practical leadership situations, Team Dynamics Card-style guidance, reflection questions, action prompts, and examples that fit your context.

The Allianz example shows how Growth Mindset 2.0 can be translated into a company-specific e-book with inspiration, guidance, leadership situations, and practical tools for leadership teams.

The same approach can be developed for your organization, using your language, priorities, leadership challenges, and internal learning needs.
