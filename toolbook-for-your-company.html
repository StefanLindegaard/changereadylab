Toolbook concept

A Toolbook for Your Company

A tailored toolbook turns Growth Mindset 2.0 from a general leadership idea into something your leadership teams can actually use.

Instead of offering generic theory, a toolbook can combine a short introduction, practical leadership situations, Team Dynamics Card-style guidance, reflection questions, action prompts, visuals, and tools that fit your organization’s priorities and leadership context.

The Allianz example below shows one version of this idea. It is written as a company-specific e-book with inspiration and guidance for leadership teams, using Growth Mindset 2.0 as the foundation.

What a toolbook can include

  • Short theory around Growth Mindset 2.0 or Human + AI
  • Leadership situations tailored to your context
  • Team Dynamics Card-style reflection and action pages
  • Practical tools and interactions from the ChangeReadyLab library
  • Questions and exercises that leaders can use with their own teams
  • Guidance for HR, People, L&D, and internal facilitators

How this helps

A tailored toolbook gives your organization something concrete to build from. It creates shared language, brings leadership development closer to real work, and gives HR and People teams practical content they can use in workshops, programs, internal facilitation, and train-the-trainer efforts.

The aim is not to create a beautiful document alone. The aim is to create a useful foundation for learning, discussion, and action across leadership teams.
