Tools & Interactions

Practical tools for leadership teams

ChangeReadyLab is being built around practical tools and interactions that help leaders move from reflection to action. Some tools are individual starting points. Others are designed for leadership teams, HR and People teams, and internal facilitators.

The idea is simple. Use the tools one by one, or combine them into tailored learning programs for your organization.

Popular topic

Expand the Comfort Zone

This is one of the most useful entry points for Growth Mindset 2.0. Instead of telling leaders to “leave the comfort zone,” the focus is on understanding where people feel safe, where fear shapes behavior, and how capability can be expanded step by step.

Expand the Comfort Zone

An individual tool that helps leaders identify where they operate from comfort, fear, learning, or growth, and what practical move would help them expand capability in real work.

Diagnostics

Assessments

Assessments help make leadership behavior and change readiness visible. The value is not the score itself. The value comes from seeing patterns, gaps, and differences in perception that can lead to better conversations and clearer action.

Gap Map

A practical assessment and dialogue tool for leadership teams. It helps teams compare current and desired state across selected indicators and see where alignment is strong or weak.

What we are building next

The library will grow around a few recurring formats: Leadership Situations, diagnostics, Team Dynamics Cards, action builders, and tools that help HR and People teams create tailored learning programs.

The longer-term direction is a click-and-mix library where organizations can select tools and interactions, combine them into a learning program, and adapt the content to their own leadership context.
