Train-the-Trainer

Build internal capability to teach, facilitate, and scale

Train-the-Trainer is a key part of ChangeReadyLab. The aim is to help organizations learn and grow from the inside, using their own people, context, and capability.

Why this matters

Many leadership development efforts create useful moments, but they do not always build internal capability. HR and People teams often need practical ways to keep the work alive after a workshop, program, or external session ends.

Train-the-Trainer helps your organization develop the people who can run, adapt, and improve learning programs internally. This makes the work more sustainable and more connected to the real culture, language, and challenges of the organization.

How we build capability

We work with a small group of internal facilitators, HR partners, People leaders, or selected leadership development colleagues. Together, we build understanding, practice facilitation, adapt tools, and create confidence in using the materials with leadership teams.

This can include learning sessions, coaching, co-facilitation, practice rounds, feedback loops, and support in adapting tools to your own language and priorities.

What Train-the-Trainer can include

  • Introduction to Growth Mindset 2.0 and Human + AI perspectives
  • Facilitator guidance for using tools and interactions
  • Practice in running Team Dynamics Card-style conversations
  • Co-design of tailored learning programs for your leadership teams
  • Coaching and feedback for internal facilitators
  • Support in building a repeatable internal rhythm for learning and application

Outcome

Your organization builds the ability to use these tools and learning programs with less dependency on external support. The work becomes more integrated, more repeatable, and more relevant to your own leadership context.

Who this is for

HR and People teams, L&D teams, internal facilitators, leadership development professionals, and selected leaders who want to help others grow through practical learning experiences.
